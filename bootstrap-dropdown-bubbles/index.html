Bootstrap 3.3.7 Dropdown Bubbles

Bootstrap Tooltips and Popovers point to their caller with arrows. Modals have backdrops. Why not dropdowns? While we're at it let's rotate the trigger carets in concert with the dropdown menu settling into view. This solution includes dropup and dropdown-menu-right compatibility; as well as a darker dropdown backdrop that fades in ala modals.
